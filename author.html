a meal that heals...

As a busy and hungry college student, I often seek dinner recipes that are easy and quick to make, but also taste good and remind me of the Korean food I eat at home. This rice bowl is the perfect meal for anyone like me, who craves savory and comforting asian cuisine without wanting to put in extensive cook or prep time. A spin off of gyeranbap, it's a simple, but delicious, combination of accessible ingredients that can be ready to eat in under 20 minutes.
